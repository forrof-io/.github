## AI Development Services

### Agentic AI & Multi-Agent System Development

We design and build autonomous AI agents and multi-agent architectures that handle complex, multi-step business processes without manual intervention. Our agentic AI solutions go beyond simple chatbots — we build systems where AI agents reason, plan, collaborate, and execute across your workflows.

**What we build:**
- Autonomous AI agents for customer support, operations, and decision-making
- Multi-agent orchestration systems using LangGraph and CrewAI
- Human-in-the-loop agent workflows with escalation and approval chains
- Tool-using agents that integrate with your APIs, databases, and third-party services

### LLM Integration & RAG Pipeline Engineering

We integrate large language models into your products and internal tools with retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) pipelines that ground AI responses in your proprietary data — reducing hallucinations and delivering accurate, context-aware outputs.

**What we build:**
- Custom RAG pipelines with vector database indexing (Pinecone, Weaviate, Qdrant)
- Document intelligence systems for parsing, summarizing, and querying unstructured data
- Fine-tuned and prompt-optimized LLM integrations (OpenAI, Anthropic Claude, Google Gemini)
- Enterprise knowledge bases with semantic search and conversational interfaces

### AI Strategy Consulting & Proof-of-Concept Development

Not sure where AI fits into your business? We run discovery workshops, identify high-impact automation opportunities, and build rapid proofs-of-concept to validate feasibility before you commit to a full build.

**What we deliver:**
- AI readiness assessments and technology roadmaps
- Rapid PoC development (typically 2–4 weeks)
- Build-vs-buy analysis for AI tooling and infrastructure
- ROI modeling and business case development for AI initiatives

### Domain-Specific Chatbots & Workflow Automation

We build intelligent chatbots and automated workflows tailored to your industry — from legal intake bots and logistics coordination agents to food-ordering assistants and real estate lead qualification systems.
